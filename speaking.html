Who Is Shawn

Not Inspiration
— Truth.

Shawn Livingston is a combat veteran whose journey took him from serving his country, to injury, to heroin addiction, and ultimately to recovery.

His story is documented in 100 Miles to Redemption: a raw and unfiltered look at what it means to fall completely and choose to rise anyway.

Today Shawn lives with discipline, purpose, and intention. He channels the mental resilience that carried him through recovery into ultra running and the greatest work of his life: service. Shawn speaks to organizations and communities that want a voice rooted in lived experience, not inspiration porn, but honest, raw truth that moves people to act.

Topics
Recovery & Addiction — From the Inside Out
Military-to-Civilian Transition
Discipline, Mental Resilience & Second Chances
Community as a Recovery Tool
The Power of Storytelling in Healing
Purpose-Driven Leadership
