Our Mission

No Past

I Am Redemption was created to prove that no past is too broken and no person is beyond hope. We support individuals and communities impacted by addiction, incarceration, trauma, and loss.

Founded by combat veteran and ultra runner Shawn Livingston, whose story is documented in 100 Miles to Redemption.
