Our Foundation

Why We Exist.

I Am Redemption is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Every dollar donated goes directly to funding recovery-focused programming, community events, and outreach.

We exist because the system fails people at the moment they need it most. We are not the system. We are the community it missed.

Through donor support, community participation, and shared experience, we continue to grow impact, one story, one event, one time at a time.
