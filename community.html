Submit Your Story

Your Story
Could Save
Someone's Life.

Every story we share is proof that redemption is real. You don't have to have it all figured out. You just have to be honest.

We share stories on the podcast, in our content, and across the community. Your voice matters, and there's someone out there who needs to hear exactly what you've been through.

Stories can be shared anonymously. We review every submission personally.

Volunteer

Give Your
Time. Change
a Life.

Volunteers are the backbone of I Am Redemption. From showing up at events to helping run meetings, every hour you give goes directly to strengthening the community.

We work with individuals at every stage of recovery and are committed to creating a safe, structured environment. All volunteers complete a volunteer agreement to ensure consistency and care.

No experience required. Just a willingness to show up.

Event Support
Help set up and run community runs, Monday meetings, and special events across Austin.
Outreach & Mentorship
